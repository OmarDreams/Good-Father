Madandaulo Ga Mundu Jwankulungwa

Baba asadandawulaga pa indu ingapo,ine mwainduyo ni wanache. Ma program gawo gosope,walinganyaga ya wanache. Nambo vuto jawanache wakwete wangatenda yaganisisye baba,chisanzo chake mwanya Zaynab,wapele mpamba kut atendele business nambo wakanile mmalo mwakwe waje kutanda masengo. Ligongo lakuti baba nganalijiganya yakuwecheta yawo kwawanache isaonekaga mpela yangapikanika,mwanache kut mpaka akulupilile ikusawusya.

Pana wandu wane wasatendaga indu kuti agambe koneka kwa wandu,nipana wane asatendaga indu kut apate profit. chakwenela kuti mundu amanyilile nchakut "mundu jwakonangaga ndawi ". Mundu indu yakutenda impeje answer nsyene kuti ana akupindula chichi. Mundu ikusasosegwa kut ulinganye maganisyo,naga uganisisye ukaiwonaga kokowa. Baba yawaganisyaga nambosoni kumbila ni "kamusyana monga family", nambo naga ukusembana nago malangizoga achakulungwa kulungwa watite "talisongonele pelungo".

Uwwe pakutama tuwwendeje Nlungu kut tuwe wandu wakulidalila kulekangana nikudalila. Nlungu asasakaga umanyilileje kut ugwejo nikapolo,pauli kapolo niuwendeje kwa Nlunguko. lisiku lililose uwwendeje Nlungu antamusya ichindu ilinkukujendela ngaleka kuwenda ligongo ngaukumanyilila lisiku lawawisi Allah kuti ichikusawusya.

Chisanzo; a Khama lathu wawugwile bakery. Ali awugwile wasosile mundu jwakuimanya ya bakery nikujigala mwanache mwanache jwawo nikujakuwwika kukoko. Vuto jaliji mwanache jwawo jwakonanga,mwanache jwakamusyanaga ma plan gakonanga ni mundu jwamasengo. Wapungulaga mlingo wakwenela kuti ichindu ya mu bakery ipochele monga; flour jikusosegwa jwakwanila 50 bags wasumaga jwannono mbiya sineso nikulyaga. Sambano a khama Nlungu ali asosile kuti nombe nawo awe mmavuto, mmalo mwakwe chilichosye chaku bakery watendaga control asyene. Watiga kusumisya indu yamu bakery mbiya sajalaga kwawomwanjawo. Mapeto gakwe bakery jagwile, soni shop chawo nganichiwa chenene (chajalaga pasi) kutochela pelepo shop chawele chili ngachikwenda mpaka lelo.

Chiganyo nchakuti; Mundu uli nkusaka kugula ndambi jine ja business,mpamba wawukamulichisile masengo pakugula ndambijo uwe uwowo. Naga kuli kwele kutocheleje pelepo naga kuli kugwa ikakutenda soni affect ugwe ku business jakwe ja kala.
