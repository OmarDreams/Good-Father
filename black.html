Vuto Jawandu Wachikuda

Ntundu uliwose ukwete mavuto gakwe: kuchikuda nombe kukwete mavuto

Vuto jawandu wachikuda kusekana. Mundu naga ali atesile chindu nikanika kangala kunseka. Ndawi sejinji indu mundu naga ikulemile wandu asatiga ikaniche ligongo wangalijiganya kapena ligongo lakut wangali lunda,kutendaga kuti nganawa wachikudape wanti kutenda chindu ningakomboleka. Baba agaweni ma estate gamajinji gazungu galigawugalidwe. agamanyisoni ma company gamajinji gawandu walipende lechejewu gali gawugalidwe. Mwachisanzo wakwendesa company ja universal lelo,nganawa wawowo wawajendesaga soni liso.

Company ja cundlex wawatandikasisye niwane apano wakwendesa soni wane. Kanika chindu nganijiwa mbali jakuti ugwejo wangali lunda,nambo isawaga kuti Nlungu nganakupa jele mbalijo. Isagambaga kuwa kuti ugwejo ngawukumanyilila mbali jakwe . Nlungu mundu julijose wansagulile kawe, antamusya ulijiganye chantuli Nlungu asagambaga kukupa ulesi nigambaga tama mpela mundu jwangalijiganya,nikulagaga soni.Nipana wandu wane akusakombolekaga nikuwa wangalijiganya nambo ligongo lakuti Nlungu wapele baraka jajijinji tusimane akwete katundu jwajujinji.

Twakete mwanja Sawuzande; waliji wangali antamusa aaa nambo wakwete ma truck 20 chakuti, wakwete imajumba mulilongwe nambo apano ite kumala niwanache. Uwwe tukwete vuto,wangasosa kupitilisya chiwaganisisyaga makolo. Uwwe tukusasaka jwalijose ayitande yakwe,nambo azungu asawaga alinkulola ipepala yawalembile makolo gawo kalakalako!!! nakusawaga alinkwendela yalakweyo.

Chisanzo azungu ali atemi pasi wachijigelle chilambo cha Africa kut chikajala pasogolo.yalakweyo niyayikutendedwaga magano. Ndawi jelejo wandu wachikuda waliji wangajala ku school nambo wakombolaga kulinganya wuti. Jele ndawijo ilombo ilinkogoya ligongo lakut chilambo chino chatupile matinji. Wasitendaga wuti sakwe Namgogodela. Ndawi sine ligongo lakusingalilwa, wajigalaga maganga nikugajocha nikupanganya lijela,ndawi sine chipula (lina lakwe Lwembe). Achinangolo ali asingalilwe kuti moto tuwupate uli,wajigalaga chitela lina lakwe Kaliwuti M'bwabwa; chaliji chitela changalimba kaneko niwajigalaga chitela chine chakulimbangana nikupowolela chitela changalimba chila nikupechesyaga.

Sambano ali ayiche a Kamuzu walamwile kut ngakusaka mundu jwampiliwu kupanganya wuti. Wapatikene niwuti ndawi jelejo wawulajidwaga kapena kutawidwa. Wandu wawakwetewo wagambaga kwasa,kaneko lunda lwakupanganyila wuti lwamalile papopo. Mavuto gane gatukwete uwwe wandu wachikuda; mundu antamusa akole mbiya sejinji uli wangakumbila kola machine napanganyeje katundu. M'mangochi muno kapena kuti mmalawi muno pana wandu wakwete makobili gamajinji,wane akwete ma 500,000,000 wane ma 2 billione wane ma 5 billione. Nambo mbiya sila wangakombolaga kuti atende chindu chekulungwa.

Jele nivuto jatukwete wandu wachikuda,wawoga kutenda chindu chekulungwa nikuliwika soni kuti nganimba ngwanisye. Mundu jwachikuda jwaulesi jwakusangalala nikutumidwa. Mundu pa umi wakwe nganimba ukwanisye yosope, chimozimozi umbuli wangakomboleka kujigala magawo gosopene. Indu yamundu ilinkusaka kwima, yangajimaga ligongo lakuti mundujo ngakuimanyilila iyayi, ikusajimaga antamusa mundujo chinducho ali achimanyi. Allah asagambaga kulamula kuti iwe yanti niyikusawaga iyoyo basi. chimozimozi mundu indu ilinkwenda isawaga kut Nlungu pejo agambile kusaka kuti ikujendele. Kwana ndawi Allah asajimika lunda lwamundu lulichiwela.

Chisanzo chawandu watesile mistake ali walijiganye chili mpela cha Samu Mpaso ni Yusuf Matumula. Yusuf Matumula wakwete nganisyo sakusaka kwasima mbiya,wakamulene ni a Samu Mpaso (nduna ja boma jele ndawijo) kuti Yusuf Matumula ajasime mbiya ku bank nambo chikole chakwe chiwe nyumba ja Samu Mpaso. ili ikomboleche, nikupita ndawi a Yusuf Matumula indu yaje kwasawusya mpaka kulepela kuwusya ngongole ku bank kula. yapatikene kuti a Samu Mpaso waje kusumulwa nyumba jawo ligongo lakuti chikole chaliji nyumbajo.

Chiganyo: Kulijiganya kwanga ntendekasya mundu kut akapata mavuto. Ndawi jakwika vuto jangalola kulijiganya kwa mundu. Nambo wandu ndawi jagakwika mavuto kwa mundu jwangalijiganya,wandu asalolaga ungalijiganya wa mundujo. Chekulungwa nlope uweje mundu jwakuwwenda Nlungu musogolo jakwe. Ligongo ndawi sine isawaga mpela ukongolela nichili chiyambi chakonanga. Nlungu ali asosile kumasya indu ya mundu ndawi sine asatosaga lunda,nambosoni Nlungu ali asosile kunkwesa mundu asampaga nganisyo sambone.
