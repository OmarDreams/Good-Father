Umbone Wa Malonda

Business nimasengo bola business. wandu wapamasengo asagambaga kuoneka kuchimbichika ndawi jakulawa kumasengo pejo basi Nambo ali atite apumule masengogo,umi wawo usaoneka kuti wakulaga. Mwachisanzo mundu jwakwiganya m'boma ali atite apumule kapena kutenda retire asajalaga soni pa private. kulosya kuti makobili gawapochelaga ali m'boma nganigawa gampaka kutendela chitukuko chakuti uli ulesile masengo nikudalilaga pelepo.

Mundu jwa police kapena jwa army ili insawisye ndawi sine asakombolekaga kutenda ya security pa malo ganikwe (akwete kuwa mlonda pa malo panepakwe). Ndawi sejinji salary jamundu jwapamasengo jisawaga profit ja mundu jwa business palisiku limo. Mundu umanyilileje kuti apa ndili pambone kapena nginimba pambone. Naga mundu uli pambone ningamanyililaga kuti uli pambone usakombolekaga kujakwima pa nzele wa wandu wakuwenda.

Ichindu yekulungwa kulungwa M'malawi muno achimisyene wakwe niwandu wa business,ma factory gamajinji nambo soni mashop gekulungwa kulungwa achimisyene wakwe wandu wa business ngawaga wamasengo. Baba wajamukaga a Sharyf kuti "chilambochi chikwenda,papali ndawi jine jakwe une (baba) naliji mwanache nambo ndawi ajino nimwanguwonechelamu (wakulungwa kuyaka nambo soni wandu wajinji asadalilaga omwanjawo".

Business jisampaka ufulu mundu wakuya malamusi ga nlungu monga Swala,zakat,hajji,niyineniyine. kupitila mu business mundu ukwete kutenda yekulungwa mundawi ja nnono monga kusuma galimoto . Yaka yamunyuma mundu jwapamasengo nganakolaga mpata wakusuma galimoto.
