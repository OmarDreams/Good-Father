Yakumbila Ya Mundu Jwankulungwa

Khumbo la Baba wakumbisile kuti atende center jakusana yachipembeo. kaneko waje pwatikanya,center ja deen ni school jakwe. nambo yosopeyo ngani ijala pasogolo. Yalakweyi wakumbilaga pakukulola kumangwawo mwakwawelele (kumusi) kumbali ja chipembezo. wakumbilaga kuti akole center jawo akajongolele mwawasoselaga. Nambo wangadandawula nlope ungapitilila wa Khumbo lawolo ligongo ali m'gulu ja history. walinjilile.

Chindu chine chasakumbilaga Baba ni factory,umi wawo asakumbilaga uwwe mpela family tulinkupanganya katundu. chindu chine chawele alinkuchisachilila ni kulanga nguku,awele alinkusachilila Yalakweyi ligongo welewo nimpata wakutukuchila business ligongo lakuti atupule wandu wakulyaga nguku magano nambosoni nganatupa wakusunjilaga ngukusyo.

Ligongo lakuti pa town ja Mangochi pano pana mundu jumo jwakulichisya nguku (wali mayi KHANI) ma plan gakwete baba nigakusinda malo ga ku ndata kuti gawe itundu yanguku. Nambope kulanga nguku nimasengo gekulungwa gakusosegwa kuti nsyene aweje papopo kapena kuyikuyagakuyaga. Ligongo lakuti wamasengo wane asakombolekaga kwiwa nguku kapena akwete kujiwulaga mele nikulambuchisya kut jiwile jika chakulinga chakuti ajitume. Naga mundu ukusaka kupate jwamasengo ulolechesye kuti upateje jwambone jwakulupichika . Apano mashop gatupile niyakusosegwa kolaga yakutenda ingapo.

Khumbo jine asakumbilaga jwalijose pa famaly petu ali akwete yakutenda nitamaga malo gamo kamusyanaga indu. yasatendaga Nlungu ulinkwakamuchisya wandu nombe ugwe wanache wakwe tachikola wakwakamuchisya ili yasawisye. Nombe uwwe soni tukwenela kumanyilila kuti masengo ga mbiya nikwakamuchisila wandu wali mmavuto. Masengo gambiya nikusumila Janna. Kumbali jababa chakusachiona chambone champaka atende mundu ni business. Business usakomboleka kwinjisa mbiya sannono nikopoka profit jejinji. chakulinga cha baba pakutandikasa school jila chaliji chakut awoneche mwanache jumo jwatakatame pelepo.

Mundu ukakuonaga unandi kwakukutochele lusulo,upakusyeje pelepo,upachimbichisyeje ligongo lakuti ugwe kut uyiche paulipo utochele papopo. Nambo ugambe kusosa litala kuti "ana lwele lusulolu ngalukula ligongo chichi". Nganiwa yambone yakujileka business nikuleka kunkamuchisya jwakukamuchisye kuti ugwe ulijiganye. Vuto jetu tusaiwonaga unandi yayikoneka ni meso.
