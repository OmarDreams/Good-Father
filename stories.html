Stories of those who tried and failed

Baba asatiga kut omwanjawo nganajika wawatite kulinga indu ni ngakomboleka.

Papali a JOHN TEMBO mundawi ja MCP. A Tembo ali mu mcp ndawi jelejo wakwete machili nlope makamaka ali nkulamulila a Kamuzu. Watiga aTembo naga ali awechete chindi a Kamuzu wakamulichisaga masengo.Wawele ali nkuwulaga wandu wajinji kupitila mukwalambuchisila indu yangalewa. pakuwusaka usogoleli walongolaga kulola wandu wayikwajendela muchipanimo kapena kut wandu wakwete sogolo nikwawulagaga.

Wandu wawulajidwe kupitila mwa a m'dalawo waliji mwanja Muwalo.Otoni Chirwa.Elon Gadama.Twaibu Sangala ni Dick Matenje. Nambo yakulinga yawo nganiikomboleka. a Kamuzu pawatendaga resign wawisile a Bakili mpela nsogoleli.

A Bakili mu mcp waliji mpelaga secretary-general. watesile resign nikutanda ja transportation. wakwete minibus lina lakwe JAKUMUSI BUS SERVICE. Jajesile ndawi jamnono kaneko watandite ya ndale kaneko nikujakuwa president.

Jonus Savimbi

chisanzo chine chawandu wanti kutenda chindu ningakomboleka nicha Jonus Savimbi. Kumangwawo kwaliji kwa Angora.Wakwete chipani lina lakwe UNITA. Awele ali nkutama mwitinji kwandawi jelewu pa mpepe nigulu jawo aku alinkulimbana ni Boma jamuAngora. Atemi mwitinjimo kwandawi jelewu mpaka ayikene kuwa yawasakaga ngakomboleka.

Chakufwa Chihana

A Chihana ni mundu jwandanda kutchuka M'malawi muno kumbali ja multiparty a Bakili nkanawe kumanyika. chawatesile kut achuke wayiche pa Ndege kut mwine ilandane nimwawawichile a Kamuzu.Nambo kaneko akamuzu wala watawile a Chihana. A Chihana ali atawidwe munyumamu wawaliji pampata ni a Bakili Muluzi.A Bakili niwawasungwile nlope mwakwanila kwakopa wandu kut akunde multi-party. Kaneko Boma jajitiche multi-party gala nambo pele a Chihana ali mundende.Ndawi jiwatandaga campaign nipawagopolaga a Chihana wala nambo pele a Bakili ali amanyiche kala.

Ali apikisene pa u president yapatiche kut wawinile a Bakili Muluzi nikuwa president jwa Malawi.Term jawili ali apikisene soni a Chihana waluzi soni. Pele nipawatandaga wandu wakumpoto kocha misikiti ligongo lakut wakulamulilawo (Bakili) waliji Nsilamu.Wajochiche misikiti jakwana 17. Upile asilamu nganasaka kuwuchisya pawele ntopolawo. Pawapepile ntima wandu wakumpoto wala wandu watandite kulinganya soni misikit jila.A Bakili ali ayiweni kut ndale sipanganyisye chidani wachiwendile chilambo kut a chihana awe wachiwili kwa wachiwili (second vice president). Ali atesile yele yasimaniche kut chilambo chitandite kwunjila ntendele.

Pamapeto payosope a Chihana mpaka lelo nganaupateje u president. walinjilile mwiwalinjilile mosemo mambo nganiikomboleka.

Raphosa Jakama

Papali soni Raphosa Jakama wakutochela ku Mozambique.watesile ngondo ndawi jelewu pafupifupi 17 years alinkulimbana nichilambo.kut wele awe Nsogoleli nambo mpaka wawile yawasakaga ngakomboleka.
