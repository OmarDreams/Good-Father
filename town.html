CHIYAMBI CHA TOWN JA MANGOCHI

Muwulendo wawo wakusama kutochela ku Malembo kwala M'Mangoch.wawichile pa Mankhwala chapajili fueling station m'chaka cha 1994. awele alinkutenda business gele malogo kwa nyengo jelewu nambo business nganijajendelaga. Kaneko waganisisye yakuwenda malo kwa Assembly. Sele ndawiso malo galiji gauwlele (ndawi jagamasile malo wa Assembly wantendaga mundu akasume malo kaneko apate ipepala ya Assembly).

Muchanasa cha Nlungu wa Assembly wala malo gakutama. Malo gawo galiji mwakulolegana ni ofesi ja UDF. sele ndawiso kuli kwana matinjitinjipe. Kwaliji kuli kwangali chakutawidwa chilichose chawachisimene kupatula ofesi ja UDF. Ofesi ja UDF Kundanda jaliji kitchen jawa chipatala. Azimmayi wakwembechela wagonaga ku chele chi jumbacho. Pajele ndawijo chijumbacho chatawiwidwe mpela chitukuko cha Boma.

Nyumba jine jawajisimene jaliji ja HotPot. Ndawi jelejo jili mwanyumbape nkanawe kutawa hotel. Nyumba jine jalijija chipatala cha Banja La M'sogolo. pachiyaambi kumeso kwa Banja la msogolo kwaliji kapena kut kwalolite chakwagali ma shop ga a Ngoni(lina la mundu). Baba shop chawo chandanda wachitawile mwakulolegana ni ofesi ja UDF. kwaliji kuli kwangali shop jilijose mmtawuni ja Mangoch kusungulila malo gawayichile Baba go jele ndawijo kupatula shop chawopecho basi.

Omwanjawo niwawatandikasisye nsewu wakutochela chakumashop ga Kambulile kwamwanja Ntusule. Wawutandikasisye nsewuwo kupitila mukwawenda wa Banja la msogolo kut awiche chikwangwani chachilanjileje ku Banja la msogoloko kut mwine itendekasyeje nsewuwo kuwa wa busy.
