Malangizo Ga Business

Chakulinga cha BUSINESS nikupanganya profit (mbiya japachanya) jakut mundu jikukamuchisye kwanilisya yawukusosa pa wakwe. yeleyi kuti ikomboleche mundu usasosegwa indu ingapo. ine mwainduyo nimonga kugamanya malangizo ga business. Chandanda pa business pasasosegwa kuti mbiya sisunguleje.Nakalaga wandu wachimwenye asakondaga kutupiya yakutenda.

Baba waweni wandu wawatandite ni mbiya sannono nambo apano nima billionaire. Wane wasumisyaga nguwo samalitaka pajele ndawijo nambo apano akwete company jakulinganya soya pieces jwa FASA.Walesile shop nikujakutanda factory.Pana soni wane wawatendaga yanguwo peyo nambo apano akwete company jakupanga indu katundu jwakulekangana lekangana. Walakwewo niwasapanganyaga soap jwa greenlight,mawuta ga clere nambo soni girlFriend,ni indu ine niyine. Yangapinjila mundu kuti uyimanye yosope kuti ukole company,mundu ukusakolaga experience ja indu makamaka naga induyo ikuwandichile.

Chawili business jisawengana nikulipikana. Mundu naga uwugwile shop ulitendeje kut ugwejo nikapolo,anta ali ayiche mwanache unchimbichisyeje. Papali M'wewnye jwine jwakwe ku Limbe,blantyer. Jwakwete shop nambo shop chakwe chagwile ligongo lakulipikana. Mbiya jamwana nlope jachipepala pajele ndawijo jaliji 5 kwacha,mbiya jekulungwa jaliji 100 kwacha. Sambano amwenye wajinji ilinkwajendela asatiga "ndikufuna ndalama wankulunkulu". Kweleku kusawaga kuyelekedwa,asatiga ikupeleka busy.

M'mwenye jwalakwejo chawatesile ali ayiweni kut wandu wane akwikaga nimbiya samwanamwana,wawisile mundu panlango wakwinjilila mu shop. Masengo gakwe mundujo galiji gakwawusya wandu (ma customer) kut ana ajigele ntundu wachi wambiya.Naga customer jula jujanjile kuti jujigele ma 5 kwacha kapena ma 10 kwacha wantendaga akajinjila.Baba yeleyo wayiweni ni meso. Ali atandite yeleyo ma customer gatandite kujila munyuma panandipanandi. kaneko patandite kupita ndawi jelewu pali pangali customer. Ndawi sine mwine mpaka 30 minus kaneko mwine mpaka 1 hour kaneko mpaka shop chawugalidwe.Ngani jakwe katundu jupali nambo gamba tenda mistake usasokonesyaga yejinji. Ni mundu jwa business ukakulaga ntima, umanyilileje kut mabwana niwakwikaga nago ma 50 kwachawo.

chindu chine pa business isasosegwa 3 to 4 years ugwejo uweje millionaira kapena billionaire (mukusosa kwa nlungu).kumbali jakuoda katundu ulongoleje kuwalanjila gaje katundu jula juli juyiche pa nyumba kut ulole mwawujendele,umanyilileje kut ana akujiwile kapena iyayi. Ligongo ku order naga ngakujigala chenene usakatikaga nako.Baba ajiwilidwepo ni a Mwenye ndawi singapo ku order.
