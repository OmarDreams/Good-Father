Introduction of the great man

Wapagwile ku Mangochi mmusi wa Ngo'mbo.Wapgwile muliwasa lakulaga nlope. Liwasa langali ichindu yakoneka ni meso.

Kupita kwandawi Allah wapele kulimbichila muyakutenda yawo. alimile ganyu jakupweteka nlope kwa ndawi jelewu. Ganyu sekulungwa kulungwa nambo malipilo gakwe ma 2 kwacha.

Baba nganafooka. Wakwete meso gakulola patali. Wasamile ku Ngo'mbo (kumangwawo) nikwawula ku Malembo (Lina la musi m'boma jijojo ja Mangoch) kusosa makobili. Kundanda wagambaga kwawula ku Malemboko kutenda business nikujaga

1986 ni chaka chawapite kutenda ndamo pampepe ni liwasa lawo. Kumalembo atemiko yaka yakwana 8. Walingaga kuliwuta wuta pakusosa makobili monga kutend ma bsiness gakulekangana lekangana.Jili jipite ndawi jelewu alinkulinga linga kuti apate upile wakupata makobili. Wakwete ajawo wawpele nganisyo sakujila kumusi (ku ngo'mbo) kulima sona.

watandite kulima sona 1982. Ali asumisye jwele sonajo waje kulima soni 1993. Sona jwawalimile 1993 waje kusumisya 1994

Ali asumisye wele ulendowo .wayiwen kut asame ku Malembo akatame ku town (Mangoch town). Yele yayiche ligongo lakut kumalembo jatupaka ndawi jelewu jangasumisya. Chindu chawiri nichakut; ku Malembo monga mungulugulu mwa nyasa Azimay pakusaka kujakutka mesi m'yasa asakangalaga kwinda nguwo nguwo syawo. Sambano deen ja chisilam jachimbichisye nlope azimmayi kungani jakulosya iwalo. Yele indu iwiliyo niyayatendekasise kusama kumalembo.

Watosi kumalembo kwawula ntawuni ja Mangochi m'chaka cha 1994 pa 17 may alinkuti ntondo kuponya chisankho chawawinile a Bakili Muluzi.
