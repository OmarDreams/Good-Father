Chikondi Cha Nangolo Wetu

Maganisyo ga baba nigakupeleka malo myala mwa wanache wawo chilepe babawo ali wumi. Khumbo nilakuti induyo iwe mpela ya family ngawaga mundu jumo,yeleyi kutendela mwine isamaliche ligongo lakuti naga ili m'myala mwa mundu jumo mwine lisiku line tachiganisya yakusumisye antamusya wandu wane wa family ali ngakusaka ligongo lakuti malo ngali mmakono mwawo.

Chakupelechela chilepe baba chipanjechi nchakuti mwine patachiwaga tawe ali atulechele kala yakutenda. kumapeto kwakwe malangizo ga baba nigakuti mundu ayitalichileje yalekasisye Nlungu,kuitalichila yalekasisye Nlungu tuchiwa nsyowe jwa Nlungu.
